Match replays

Every real client submits its own replay at match end — two records sharing a matchKey are the two sides of one match.

Ready.

"Consistent" means the two sides report opposite results (or both draw) and agree on round count — the minimum you'd expect from two honest clients replaying the same match. It is not a full divergence check (that needs actually re-simulating each side's action log) — just a cheap, immediate sanity flag on the summary fields already submitted. "View raw" fetches the full stored record (settings/seed/action log) for one side on demand.
